About 11Sari

Founded in 2020, 11Sari is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Indian sarees, particularly the exquisite craftsmanship of Varanasi silks.

Our Mission

To connect saree enthusiasts with authentic, handcrafted textiles while supporting the artisans who create these treasures. We aim to keep traditional weaving techniques alive while embracing contemporary designs and innovations.

Our Story

11Sari began with a passion for the intricate art of saree weaving. Our founder, Anamika Singh, grew up surrounded by the vibrant textiles of Varanasi. After witnessing the challenges faced by local weavers, she decided to create a platform that celebrates their craftsmanship and brings their creations to a global audience.

What Makes Us Different

  • Direct relationships with master weavers and artisans
  • Commitment to fair trade practices and sustainable production
  • Blend of traditional craftsmanship with contemporary designs
  • Detailed documentation of each saree's origin and weaving technique
