The Tradition of 11 Sarees

In Indian culture, the number 11 symbolizes prosperity and new beginnings. Gifting a collection of 11 sarees to a bride is a time-honored tradition that signifies blessings for her marital journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to prepare a customized bridal gift box?

We typically require 4-6 weeks to prepare a fully customized bridal gift box. This allows sufficient time for consultation, selection, customization, and proper packaging. For urgent requirements, please contact us directly to discuss expedited options.

Can I select specific types of sarees for the gift box?

Absolutely! Our bridal gift boxes are fully customizable. You can choose specific weaving techniques, fabrics, colors, and designs to create a personalized collection. Our stylists will guide you through the selection process.

Do you offer international shipping for bridal gift boxes?

Yes, we offer international shipping for our bridal gift boxes. Additional shipping charges apply based on the destination. Each box is carefully packaged to ensure the sarees arrive in perfect condition.

Can I include jewelry or accessories in the gift box?

Yes, we can include complementary jewelry, blouse pieces, petticoats, and other accessories to complete the bridal ensemble. These can be discussed during your consultation to create a truly comprehensive gift.
