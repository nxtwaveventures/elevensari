COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our sarees, ordering process, and shipping policies.

What makes Banarasi silk sarees special?

Banarasi silk sarees are renowned for their gold and silver zari work, intricate designs, and dense silk weave. The designs often feature traditional motifs like floral patterns, paisleys, and intricate borders. Each saree takes anywhere from 15 days to 6 months to create by skilled artisans who have inherited this craft through generations.

How do I care for my silk saree?

We recommend dry cleaning for your precious silk sarees. If hand washing is necessary, use cold water with a mild detergent designed for silk. Never wring the saree; instead, gently squeeze out excess water and roll it in a towel. Air dry in shade and store in a cotton or muslin cloth, away from direct sunlight. Store with silica gel packets to prevent moisture damage.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping. International orders typically take 7-14 business days to arrive, depending on your location. We use trusted courier services with tracking capabilities to ensure your precious sarees reach you safely. Please note that customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unworn items in their original packaging with tags attached. Please note that custom-made or personalized sarees cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return process.

Are your sarees authentic handloom products?

Absolutely. Every saree in our collection is handwoven by master artisans from Varanasi. We source directly from the weavers' communities and ensure fair trade practices. Each saree comes with a certificate of authenticity detailing the weaving technique, materials used, and the artisan's information.
